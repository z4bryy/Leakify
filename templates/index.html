BIOGRAPHY

The Legend of JuiceWRLD

Jarad Higgins — known worldwide as JuiceWRLD — was born December 2, 1998 in Chicago, Illinois. A self-taught multi-instrumentalist, he could play piano, guitar, and drums by his teens. He began releasing music on SoundCloud in 2015 and exploded onto the mainstream with "Lucid Dreams" in 2018, which peaked at #2 on the Billboard Hot 100.

In just two years at the top, Juice released Goodbye & Good Riddance, Death Race for Love, and collaborated with artists from Future to Ellie Goulding. His free-associative freestyle rap style and emo-trap sound defined a generation. He passed away on December 8, 2019 — but the vault of unreleased music he left behind continues to reveal his genius.

The Emo-Trap Blueprint

Sampling Sting, interpolating The Police — Juice had an ear for melodic pain that connected with millions. His production choices redefined what hip-hop could feel like.
